About

I work on autonomous robotics: SLAM, guidance–navigation–control, and motion planning for AMRs / autonomous forklifts in complex industrial environments.

Research interests

  • 3D SLAM & localization (LiDAR-Inertial, visual-inertial)
  • Motion planning (global + local), Frenet optimal trajectories
  • Sensor fusion and robust navigation in dynamic warehouses

Current focus

Building production-grade ROS 2 autonomy stacks with scalable mapping/localization, behavior planning, and safety-oriented obstacle avoidance.
